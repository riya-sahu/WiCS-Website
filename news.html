First Meeting - 10/25/2024

We discussed the logistics of how the club was going to run this year.

At the end, we ran a miniature hackathon with prizes to demonstrate how we can develop solutions to real-world problems.

The prompt was: Create an application, website, or other program to help people become more familiar with English slang.

Congratulations to our winners! And thank you to all those who attended the meeting.
